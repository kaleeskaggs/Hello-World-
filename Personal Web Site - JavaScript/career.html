Software Developer:

In this job the median pay is $100,690 per year. The entry-level education is Bachelor's Degree. You do not have to have any work experience. You also do not have to have any on the job training. In the work environment many software developers work for firms that deal in computer systems design and related services or for software publishers.

There are many different jobs that you can do in this field. This field is all about computers and doing things with technology. Most of these jobs make quite a bt of money. If you enjoy working with technology and creating things, this could be the job for you.

Some of these jobs are a software developer, computer support specialist, computer system analysts, and information security. There are hundreds of jobs in this field. This field is growing very fast.
